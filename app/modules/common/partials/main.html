Visualising Datasets

Policy Compass platform grants users and decision makers the ability to visualise datasets by using different graphical forms, such as line graphs, pie, bar or map charts.

By giving users the freedom to apply Metrics to Datasets, Policy Compass grants us the power to describe real world situations from data. In addtion to this, users are given the opportunity to select and construct Metrics, giving us the ability to visualise data and include events that may or may not have had a profound effect on the outcome of the results of data.
